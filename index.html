About the Wedding

Celebrate love and unity in an event to remember! Join us for a magical day filled with heartfelt moments, joyous laughter, and cherished memories. Here's what you can look forward to:

What to Wear

Preferably in our wedding colors. Kindly refrain from wearing white, as it is reserved for the Bride and Groom.

Ceremony Highlights

Witness the magical union in a beautifully orchestrated ceremony featuring heartfelt vows, stunning decor, and a touch of tradition.

Parking Area

Ample parking is available for all guests during our reception area, ensuring a hassle-free arrival. Clear directions that will help guide you to your spot.

Snap and Share

Snap, Share, and Celebrate! Capture the joy, spread the love, and make the memories last forever—don’t forget to use our hashtag! #PAOLOfoundhismIRAcle

Food & Drinks

Savor an exquisite culinary experience featuring a variety of dishes and beverages. From gourmet delights to refreshing drinks, our menu caters to every palate.

Gift Guide

The couple feels incredibly blessed with all that they have and cherish your presence and prayers above all else. That is truly all they ask. However, for those who wish to honor them with a gift, a monetary contribution would be greatly appreciated. Your generosity will help them embark on their new journey together and create lasting memories.

When & Where

Celebrate this joyous occasion at a stunning venue that perfectly sets the tone for love and togetherness.
For your convenience, you can easily navigate to both locations using the MAP for directions. We look forward to celebrating with you and creating beautiful memories together!

Church

The wedding ceremony will take place at St. Augustine Parish, San Agustin, City of San Fernando, Pampanga, on January 19, 2025 at 2:30 p.m.

Reception

Afterward, we invite you to join us for the reception at Mula De Victoria, Sto. Tomas, Pampanga.

If your have question regarding on the location or about the wedding just contact us through our social media accounts. Thankyou!
